Cinema Mode

Watch together.
Feel together.

Before the film, a shared countdown — 3...2...1 — so you begin in the exact same breath. During it, spark gestures float from your side of the virtual couch to hers in real time.

No chat during the film. Sparks only — gesture-based reactions that feel physical, not textual.

Reaction Waveform — after the movie, scrub through a visual history of every moment you both reacted.

Afterglow. One sentence each about how the film made you feel. Saved forever as a Cinema Memory.
